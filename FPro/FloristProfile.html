About Us
My Beverly Hills Florist is the premier online florist for Beverly Hills and the greater Los Angeles area. We are the best place to go for local online flower delivery. Our designers seek to make their creative style blossom in each unique floral design and event production. The community has been drawn to our European flair, contemporary trends, and stunning style of floral expressions for all occasions and affairs. We strive to use a higher quality of flower than our competitors, and for this reason, we don't use cheap blooms like Daisies, Carnations or Baby's Breath, preferring the highest-quality Orchids, Roses, Peonies, Hydrangeas, Lilies, Tulips and more.
Substitution Policy
BloomNation Substitution Policy: We select the most creative florists to be in our marketplace, florists who professionally recreate the design you see based on their creative interpretation. If the florist is out of a particular decorative element, (i.e. a basket, vase, plush, etc.) the florist may substitute a similar element of equal or greater value. The "look and feel" of the arrangements featured will remain the same, with the florists paying careful attention to overall shape, size, style, and color combinations. The florist will use only the freshest flowers, and will make substitutions only when necessary, depending on seasonal and regional availability. In single-variety arrangements only, variety will take preference over color. For instance, if a florist is out of "yellow tulips," they will substitute another color of tulips, not another yellow flower. Before any major substitutions are decided on, the florist will do their best to notify the sender in advance and give them the opportunity to help with the choices made for the substitution.
